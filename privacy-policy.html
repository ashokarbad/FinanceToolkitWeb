Privacy Policy

Finance Toolkit is designed around a simple idea: your financial data is yours alone. This policy explains exactly what that means in practice — in plain language, without legalese.

Effective: 19 May 2026 · Last updated: 19 May 2026

The short version

  • We don't collect your data. No accounts, no analytics, no tracking, no ads.
  • Everything stays on your device. All your calculations, expenses, and notes live in your iPhone or iPad.
  • iCloud sync is optional and goes through your Apple account — not through our servers. We never see it.
  • Face ID / Touch ID stays on your device. Biometric data never leaves iOS.
  • Notifications are local. Scheduled by your device, not sent from any server.
  • No third-party SDKs. No Firebase, no Google Analytics, no advertising networks.

01

Scope and who we are

This Privacy Policy applies to the Finance Toolkit iOS application ("the App"), published by the developer ("we", "us", "our"). It covers what happens to data when you use the App.

The App is a personal finance utility offering calculators, expense tracking, outflow management, and payment reminders. It is intended for personal, non-commercial use.

02

Data we do not collect

The App is built to function entirely without a backend. We want to be specific about what this means:

  • We do not require sign-up, registration, or any user account.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any identifying information.
  • We do not collect your financial data — your calculations, expenses, salary, loan amounts, payment reminders, or notes never leave your device through our systems.
  • We do not use analytics tools (e.g. Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude).
  • We do not use crash reporting tools that send data to us. The App relies only on Apple's standard crash reports, which you control via iOS Settings.
  • We do not display advertisements or use advertising identifiers.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share user data — because there is no user data for us to share.
Because we do not collect personal data, requests like "delete my data" or "export my data" do not apply to us — there is nothing on our side to delete or export. Your data is entirely under your control on your own device.
03

Data stored on your device

To make the App functional, certain information is stored locally on your iPhone or iPad using Apple's standard storage mechanisms (UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device unless you choose to enable iCloud sync or export a backup. It includes:

  • Saved calculations (loan EMIs, SIP plans, FD maturities, etc.)
  • Expense entries with categories and tags
  • Monthly outflow records and salary entries
  • Payment reminders and due dates
  • Notes you create within the App
  • App preferences (selected language, currency, appearance, biometric lock toggle)

If you delete the App, this data is removed from your device. If you have enabled iCloud sync, your iCloud copy persists according to your Apple iCloud settings.

04

iCloud sync

If you enable iCloud sync (an optional feature), the App uses Apple's iCloud Key-Value Store (NSUbiquitousKeyValueStore) to synchronise your data across devices signed in to the same Apple ID.

What this means in practice:

  • Your data is transmitted directly between your device and Apple's iCloud servers.
  • We do not operate any servers and do not receive a copy.
  • The data is stored within your personal iCloud account and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • You can disable iCloud sync at any time from iOS Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud, or by signing out of iCloud.
05

Permissions we request

Face ID / Touch ID / Optic ID (Biometric Authentication)

Used only to unlock the App when biometric lock is enabled. Biometric data is handled entirely by iOS through Apple's Secure Enclave and is never accessible to us. We only receive a success/failure result.

Notifications

Used only for local payment reminders that you create within the App. Notifications are scheduled and delivered by your device. No data is sent to or from any server. You can disable notifications anytime in iOS Settings > Notifications > Finance Toolkit.

Files / Document access

Used only when you explicitly export data (PDF, CSV, JSON backup) or import a backup file. The App reads and writes files only with your direct action.

06

Third-party services

The App does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, social media, or tracking SDKs.

The only external services involved are those provided by Apple Inc. as part of the iOS platform:

  • iCloud Key-Value Store — for optional sync
  • LocalAuthentication framework — for biometric unlock
  • UserNotifications framework — for local reminders
  • App Store — for distribution and updates

These are governed by Apple's own privacy practices, available at apple.com/legal/privacy.

07

Children's privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). Since the App does not collect personal information from anyone, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a minor's data has somehow been provided to us, please contact us — though as explained above, we maintain no user data.

08

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under privacy laws such as the EU's GDPR, the UK's UK GDPR, California's CCPA/CPRA, and India's DPDP Act 2023. These typically include the right to access, correct, or delete personal data held about you.

Because we do not collect or hold any personal data, there is nothing on our side to access, correct, or delete. However, you retain full control over the data on your device:

  • To export your data: use Settings > Data Export inside the App (JSON backup).
  • To delete your data: use Settings > Reset inside the App, or delete the App from your device.
  • To delete iCloud data: sign in to your iCloud account and remove the App's data, or disable iCloud sync in iOS Settings.
09

Security

While no system is perfectly secure, we apply the following measures:

  • The App uses Apple's standard data protection on iOS, which encrypts on-device storage when your device is locked with a passcode.
  • Optional biometric / passcode lock adds an extra layer protecting access to the App.
  • iCloud sync uses Apple's encrypted transport and storage.
  • The App has no network code beyond iCloud — there is no API to attack and no server-side database that could be breached.

You are responsible for the security of your device, your iCloud account, and any backup files you export.

10

Changes to this policy

If we materially change how the App handles data (for example, if a future version adds an optional online feature), we will update this policy and revise the "Last updated" date at the top. For significant changes, we will also note them in the App's release notes on the App Store.

Continued use of the App after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
