Finance Toolkit

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 2026

Finance Toolkit is built offline-first, with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1 No Data Collection

Finance Toolkit does not collect any personal information. There are no servers, no accounts, no analytics, and no tracking of any kind.

  • No registration or sign-in is required to use the app
  • No names, email addresses, or identifiers are ever collected
  • No usage analytics or crash reports are sent to us
  • No advertising SDKs or third-party trackers are included in the app

2 Local Data Storage

All data you enter — calculations, expenses, outflows, payment reminders, and notes — is stored locally on your device and never leaves it unless you explicitly enable iCloud sync.

  • App data is stored in sandboxed local storage managed by iOS
  • Pro upgrade status is stored securely in the device Keychain, encrypted by iOS and never accessible to us
  • Smart Notes, saved calculations, and tracker data live entirely on-device

3 Face ID, Touch ID & Optic ID

Finance Toolkit supports biometric lock on the Dashboard to mask sensitive financial data. Authentication is handled entirely by Apple's LocalAuthentication framework and the device's Secure Enclave.

  • Biometric data (fingerprints, face geometry) never leaves your device
  • We never see, access, receive, or store any biometric information
  • The privacy screen on app backgrounding is also managed entirely on-device

4 Optional iCloud Sync

Finance Toolkit offers optional iCloud sync to keep your data consistent across your Apple devices. This is off by default and entirely in your control.

  • Sync uses Apple's iCloud Key-Value Store — not our servers
  • Synced data includes calculations, expenses, outflows, payments, and notes
  • Apple's privacy policy governs how iCloud handles this data
  • You can disable iCloud sync at any time in iOS Settings → Apple ID → iCloud

5 Data Backup & Export (Pro)

Pro users can export a full JSON backup of their data and re-import it at any time. Backup files are created locally on your device and shared only via iOS's standard share sheet — to a destination you choose.

  • We never receive or have access to your exported backup files
  • You have complete control over where your backup is stored

6 In-App Purchases

Pro upgrades are processed entirely by Apple's App Store via StoreKit 2. We never see or handle your payment information. Apple's privacy policy applies to all transactions.

7 Local Notifications

Payment reminders use iOS's local notification system. Notifications are scheduled on-device and are never routed through our servers — we have no visibility into your notification activity.

8 Children's Privacy

Finance Toolkit does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Because the app collects no data at all, it is safe for all ages.

9 Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10 Contact
